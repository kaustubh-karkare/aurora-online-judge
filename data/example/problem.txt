WAP to output the square of an integer.
Input : Read until the end of file. Each line contains a single positive integer less than or equal to 10.
Output : Output the square of the integer, one in each line.

<b>SAMPLE INPUT</b>
<code>
1
2
3
5
</code>

<b>SAMPLE OUTPUT </b>
<code>
1
4
9
25
</code>
